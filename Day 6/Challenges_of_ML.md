# Day 6 - Challenges of Machine Learning

## What are ML Challenges?

Machine Learning models do not always give correct results.

Many factors can affect the performance of a model.

## Common Challenges

### Poor Quality Data

If the data contains errors, missing values, or noise, the model may perform poorly.

### Insufficient Data

A small amount of data may not be enough for the model to learn patterns.

### Non-Representative Data

If the data does not represent real-world situations, the model may give incorrect predictions.

### Overfitting

The model learns the training data too well and performs poorly on new data.

### Underfitting

The model is too simple and cannot learn the patterns in the data.

### Changing Data

Real-world data can change over time, causing the model's performance to decrease.

## Real Example

A student studies only one chapter for an exam.

The student may perform well on questions from that chapter but struggle with other questions.

This is similar to Overfitting.

## Important Points

* Good data is important for good predictions.
* More relevant data usually improves performance.
* Overfitting reduces generalization.
* Underfitting leads to poor learning.
* Data quality is one of the biggest challenges in ML.

## One-Line Summary

Machine Learning faces challenges such as poor data, insufficient data, overfitting, underfitting, and changing real-world conditions.
